# PactAI

Turn digital agreements into structured, verifiable contracts.

PactAI is an AI-powered platform that converts informal agreements (WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok DMs, email, etc.) into structured contracts that can be reviewed, confirmed, and digitally signed.

Built for Enyata Buildathon 2026.

---

## 🧩 The Problem

Freelancers, creators, and small businesses frequently make agreements over chat.

When things go wrong:
- Clients ghost
- Payments stop midway
- Scope creep expands beyond agreement
- There is no formal contract

DMs are not contracts.

---

## 💡 Our Approach

PactAI adds a contract layer above digital conversations.

Users can:
- Paste chat conversations
- Upload screenshots
- Or manually input agreement terms

AI extracts key terms such as:
- Parties involved
- Deliverables
- Payment amount and structure
- Deadlines
- Obligations

Users review the extracted terms before generating a structured agreement.

---

## ⚙️ Planned Features (MVP)

- Chat paste → AI term extraction  
- Screenshot upload → AI extraction  
- Contract preview & editing  
- Digital confirmation/signature flow  
- PDF download of agreement  
- Optional payment integration (escrow model)  
- Contract storage & verification  

*Note: Feature scope may evolve during the build phase.*
